About

“The essence of cinema is editing” — Francis Ford Coppola

I’m Tony, an 18-year-old video editor. I got into editing about a year ago. It started with filming random moments with friends and putting them together so we could keep those memories. Somewhere along the way I realized I actually loved the editing part more than anything else.

I had a YouTube channel as a kid, so the creative instinct was always there. Now I’m focusing on it seriously — working on long-form content, strong hooks, motion graphics, and clean edits that keep people watching.

I mainly edit in DaVinci Resolve, and I’m always trying to improve and learn new techniques.

Some of the shorts I edited and posted across social media have reached 100K+ total views, which showed me how much good editing can make a difference — even with no budget behind it.
