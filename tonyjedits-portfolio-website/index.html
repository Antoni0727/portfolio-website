About

“The essence of cinema is editing” — Francis Ford Coppola

I'm Tony, 18 years old video editor. Long story short I got into editing in 2024. It started with filming a trip with friends and making a short vlog from it. Somewhere along the way editing part became a real obsession.

Lately I graduated highschool, so editing is my full time thing. Working on long-form and short-form content, strong hooks, motion graphics, and clean edits that keep people watching.

As a kid I had a small YouTube channel, so the passion for content was always there. Now I am the owner of a different YouTube channel, teaching people about what I learned myself about video editing.

For you it means that I understand the entire process. From the first idea to the final upload. I know what earns the click, what keeps viewers watching, and where they lose interest. That means every editing decision is made with your content's performance in mind, not just its appearance.
